# Steno Brand Research Summary

## Company Information

**Steno** is a Los Angeles-based legal technology company specializing in:
- Court reporting services
- Remote depositions (Steno Connect platform)
- AI-powered transcript analysis (Transcript Genius)
- Deferred payment solutions (DelayPay)

**Founded:** 2018  
**Founders:** Greg Hong (CEO), Dylan Ruga, Dan Anderson  
**Headquarters:** Los Angeles, California  
**Focus:** Tech-enabled litigation support services

## Brand Positioning

- **Modern & Tech-Forward:** Integration of AI and technology into traditional legal services
- **Professional & Trustworthy:** Court reporting and litigation support
- **Efficiency-Focused:** Tools designed to save time (Transcript Genius, streamlined workflows)
- **Attorney-Centric:** Built for law firms and legal professionals

## Product Ecosystem

1. **Steno Connect** - Remote deposition platform
2. **Transcript Genius** - AI transcript analysis tool
3. **DelayPay** - Deferred payment service
4. **Firm Dashboard** - Centralized management platform

## Key Messaging Themes

- Technology-enhanced traditional services
- Time-saving and efficiency
- Professional and reliable
- Modern approach to litigation support
