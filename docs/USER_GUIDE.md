# User Guide - Steno Draft

**Generate demand letters in minutes, not hours.**

Steno Draft is your firm's AI-powered assistant for creating professional demand letters. Upload case documents, generate polished drafts, refine with attorney instructions, and export Word documents â€” all while saving valuable time and maintaining firm-wide consistency.

## Getting Started

### Registration and Login

1. Navigate to your Steno Draft workspace
2. Click "Create one" to register a new account
3. Provide your full name, email address, and password
4. Click "Create Account" to join your firm's platform
5. Sign in with your credentials to access your workspace

### First Steps

1. **Upload Case Document**: Upload source documents (PDF, Word, or images) from your case files
2. **Create Demand Letter Draft**: Use AI to generate a professional draft demand letter from your documents
3. **Refine with Attorney Instructions**: Provide specific instructions to refine the letter's tone, content, or structure
4. **Export to Word**: Export the final letter as a professionally formatted Word document

## Features

### Case Document Upload

- **Supported formats**: PDF, Word (DOCX), JPEG, PNG, GIF, WEBP
- **Maximum file size**: 50MB
- **Upload method**: Drag and drop or click to select
- **Processing**: Documents are processed automatically in the background
- **Status**: You'll be notified when your case document is ready for letter generation

### AI-Powered Letter Generation

1. Upload your case documents (incident reports, correspondence, invoices, etc.)
2. Wait for document processing to complete (typically a few seconds)
3. Optionally select a firm template for consistency
4. Click "Create Demand Letter Draft"
5. Review the AI-generated professional draft

### Firm Template Management

Create and manage reusable letter templates for your firm:

- **Variable syntax**: Use `{{variable_name}}` for placeholders
- **Common variables**: `{{client_name}}`, `{{date}}`, `{{amount}}`, `{{attorney_name}}`
- **Template application**: Templates are applied during letter generation
- **Automatic replacement**: Variables are automatically replaced with relevant information from your case documents

**Example Firm Template:**
```
Dear {{client_name}},

This letter is regarding the matter dated {{date}}.

Sincerely,
{{attorney_name}}
```

### AI Refinement

Refine your demand letter drafts with specific attorney instructions:

- "Make the tone more formal and professional"
- "Add more detail about the property damage"
- "Strengthen the legal arguments in paragraph 3"
- "Emphasize the client's financial losses"
- "Shorten the letter while maintaining all key points"

### Real-time Collaboration

- **Multiple attorneys**: Collaborate on the same letter draft simultaneously
- **Active users**: See which attorneys are currently viewing or editing
- **Live synchronization**: Changes are synchronized in real-time
- **Change notifications**: Get notified when other attorneys make updates

### Export to Word

1. Click "Export to Word" in the letter editor
2. Download link opens in a new tab
3. Download expires after 1 hour for security
4. Word document includes professional formatting ready for client use

## Best Practices

1. **Document Quality**: Upload clear, readable case documents for optimal AI results
2. **Firm Templates**: Create firm-specific templates to standardize demand letters across your practice
3. **Review Process**: Always review AI-generated content before finalizing and sending to clients
4. **Refinement Instructions**: Use specific, clear instructions for best refinement results (e.g., "Make paragraph 2 more assertive" rather than "make it better")
5. **Collaboration**: Coordinate with team members when collaborating on drafts to avoid conflicting edits

## Troubleshooting

### Case Document Processing Unsuccessful
- Verify file format is supported (PDF, Word, or images)
- Check that file size is under 50MB
- Ensure document is readable and not corrupted
- Try uploading the document again

### Letter Generation Issues
- Verify case document was processed successfully (check status notification)
- Ensure document contains relevant legal content (incident reports, correspondence, etc.)
- Try refining with more specific attorney instructions
- Contact your system administrator if issues persist

### Export Issues
- Ensure letter draft is saved (check "Last saved" indicator)
- Verify internet connection is stable
- Try exporting again
- If download link expires, export again to generate a new link

## Support

For issues or questions, contact your firm's system administrator or Steno Draft support team.
